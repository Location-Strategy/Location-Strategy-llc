About Us

Our firm specializes in high-level real estate advisory, rooted in a foundation of transactional experience and comprehensive market analysis. Successful real estate strategy requires more than just looking at a spreadsheet; it requires understanding the 'why' behind the 'what.'

We utilize a data driven approach to analyze performance while applying the nuanced understanding of human behavior to optimize the utility and appeal of the built environment. These nuances lead to notable outperformance of one real estate asset vs another from vacancy to rents to sales price to turnover. We know that data has limitations that necessitate relationships and surveying industry, government, economic development professionals for market color. From commercial developments to residential portfolios, we deliver the insights necessary to navigate today’s complex real estate ecosystem.

Services We Offer

We provide custom consultancy or standard market feasibility studies for government special tax districts, bonds, and lender required studies. We provide studies for ground up development or existing asset analysis in single asset class projects, mixed use and master planned communities.

Market Studies and Feasibility Analysis Services:

  • City and MSA level analysis of income, jobs, and demographics
  • Drivers of job and population growth relating to household formation
  • Overview of the Retail, Commercial and Residential Landscape
  • Competitive Market Area analysis for multiple asset types including Asset comparables
  • Demand and Absorption forecasting
  • Determine the highest and best use real estate or mix of real estate based on opportunities in the trade area. This includes an analysis of subtypes within the real estate type, and potential timing on what stage to introduce specific types to the market.
