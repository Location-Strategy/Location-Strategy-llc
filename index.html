What We Do

Providing feasibility analysis for ground-up or infill development. We support builders, banks, and private equity from concept through bond issuance.

Development Planning

Project Feasibility

Services We Offer

Standard Market Study

  • City/MSA level analysis of income, jobs, and growth
  • Drivers of population growth and household formation
  • Retail, Commercial and Residential landscape overview
  • CMA Analysis (Transit, Facilities, Safety, Education)
  • Multi-family vs. Single-family trend impact analysis
  • Demand & Absorption forecasting included

Standard Rental Analysis

  • 10-20 Comparable evaluation & neighborhood mapping
  • Pricing, concessions, and vacancy rate summaries
  • Construction pipeline & impact of new deliveries
  • Feature/Amenity matrix & building photography
  • Proximity analysis to freeways, grocery, and transit

Special District Creation

Specialized analysis for MUD, LID, PID, TIRZ, and TIF areas.

  • All Standard Market Study content
  • Detailed assessed value forecasting
  • District-specific growth modeling

Market Study Visualization

  • Drone and ground photography of project site
  • Mapped visuals of community work/play ecosystems
  • MSA industry and commercial growth corridors
  • Adjacent neighborhood character documentation

Highest and Best Use

Determine the optimal real estate mix based on trade area opportunities. Includes subtype analysis and strategic timing recommendations for project stages.
