Services We Offer

We provide custom consultancy or standard market feasibility studies for government special tax districts, bonds, and lender required studies. We provide studies for ground up development or existing asset analysis in single asset class projects, mixed use and master planned communities.

Market Studies and Feasibility Analysis Services:

  • City and MSA level analysis of income, jobs, and demographics
  • Drivers of job and population growth relating to household formation
  • Overview of the Retail, Commercial and Residential Landscape
  • Competitive Market Area analysis for multiple asset types including Asset comparables
  • Demand and Absorption forecasting
  • Determine the highest and best use real estate or mix of real estate based on opportunities in the trade area. This includes an analysis of subtypes within the real estate type, and potential timing on what stage to introduce specific types to the market.
