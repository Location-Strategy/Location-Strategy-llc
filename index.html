Consulting Services

Comprehensive Offerings

📊

Standard Market Study

  • City and MSA level analysis of income, jobs, growth, and demographics tracking nationally/regionally.
  • Drivers of job/population growth and their relation to household formation.
  • Overview of Retail, Commercial, and Residential Landscape.
  • Competitive Market Area (CMA) analysis for specific asset types.
  • CMA eco-system analysis: transit, community facilities, safety/crime, culture, parks, and education.
  • Standard Rental Analysis components included.
  • Single-family market trends as move-up/alternative options impacting multi-family desirability.
  • Demand & Absorption forecasting.
🏠

Standard Rental Analysis

  • Historical and Current Apartment Market Outlook for the region.
  • Comp analysis evaluating 10-20 properties (Subject or comparable neighborhoods).
  • Deep dive into pricing, concessions, and vacancy rates.
  • Impact of current apartment construction and new unit deliveries on rates/occupancy.
  • Amenity matrix (In-unit laundry, parking, storage, pet policy, shared features).
  • Photos of comparable units and building profiles.
  • Basic demographic and community access analysis (Freeways, grocery, transit).
🏛️

Special District Creation

  • All content included in the Regular Market Study.
  • Custom assessed value forecast for specialized tax areas.
  • Specific focus on MUD, LID, PID, TIRZ, and TIF areas.
📸

Study Visualization

  • Drone and ground photography of the project site and immediate community.
  • Photography and mapped visuals of key competitors and neighborhood amenities.
  • Visual ecosystem mapping of "Work, Play, and Services."
  • Mapped visuals of key MSA industries, airport/transit hubs, and commercial growth corridors.
💡

Highest and Best Use (HBU)

Our most strategic offering, determining the optimal real estate mix based on trade area opportunities.

  • Analysis of real estate subtypes within the trade area.
  • Timing recommendations for development stages.
  • Feasibility of product mix (Residential, Retail, Office, or Mixed-Use).
  • Risk-adjusted yield optimization through site density analysis.
