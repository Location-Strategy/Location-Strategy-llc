Services We Offer

Standard Market Study

  • City and MSA level analysis of income, jobs, growth, demographics and national/regional tracking
  • Drivers of job and population growth relating to household formation
  • Overview of the Retail, Commercial and Residential Landscape
  • Competitive Market Area analysis for asset types including job centers and demographics
  • CMA analysis of ecosystem: transit, community facilities, safety, public culture, and education
  • Standard Rental Analysis components included
  • Single-family market statistics and trends impacting multi-family desirability
  • Demand and Absorption forecasting

Standard Rental Analysis

  • Historical and Current Apartment Market Outlook for region
  • Rental comp analysis of 10-20 comparables (or comparable neighborhoods)
  • Pricing, concessions, and vacancy rate data
  • Current apartment construction summary and impact of new unit deliveries
  • Matrix of features and amenities: laundry, parking, storage, elevator, and pet policy
  • Photos of comparable units and building
  • Basic demographic and community analysis (freeways, grocery, transit proximity)

Market Study: Special District

  • Same content as regular market study, plus:
  • Assessed value forecast for MUD/LID/PID/TIRZ/TIF areas
  • Specialized infrastructure impact analysis

Market Study Visualization

  • Drone and ground photography of the project site and adjacent community
  • Photography and mapped visuals of key competitors and neighborhood amenities
  • Community ecosystem mapping (Work, Play, and Services)
  • Mapped visuals of key MSA industries, businesses, and commercial growth

Highest and Best Use

Determine the highest and best use real estate or mix of real estate based on opportunities in the trade area. This includes an analysis of subtypes within the real estate type, and potential timing on what stage to introduce specific types to the market.
