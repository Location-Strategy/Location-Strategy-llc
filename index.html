History of Location Strategy

In the heart of Texas, strategic real estate solutions emerged from the vision of Scott Davis who founded the company in 2019, a veteran of the commercial real estate industry starting at a large national grocery in land acquisition to brokering land and development deals.

Location Strategy’s consulting and real estate analysis is driven by the fundamental philosophy that understanding local nuances and end users is paramount in real estate success.

We serve the largest national builders, developers, asset owners, banks, commercial brokers, lenders and private equity/capital client to smaller boutique clients. We work in multiple states and cities and leverage local expertise to provide critical insights.

What We Do

Strategic Real Estate Consulting

Location Strategy provides real estate feasibility analysis consultancy. We provide market studies to developers in ground up or infill development, builders, banks or lenders, asset owners, or private equity / debt in various phases of a project from concept or evaluating sites to in development, positioning to sell or for bond issuance.

Development Planning Services

Our experts can help with underwriting materials like pro forma, competitive market analysis, and absorption forecasts to make sure your package is ready for lender review and approval.

Project Feasibility

We help identify challenges and opportunity in the market to help you position your project as well as make recommendations on how to achieve financial targets or timelines.

Services We Offer

Standard Market Study

  • City and MSA level analysis of income, jobs, and demographics
  • Drivers of job and population growth relating to household formation
  • Overview of the Retail, Commercial and Residential Landscape
  • Competitive Market Area analysis for asset types
  • Demand and Absorption forecasting

Standard Rental Analysis

  • Historical and Current Apartment Market Outlook
  • Rental comp analysis of 10-20 comparables
  • Pricing, concessions, and vacancy rate data
  • Matrix of features and amenities: laundry, parking, storage

Market Study: Special District

  • Same content as regular market study, plus:
  • Assessed value forecast for MUD/LID/PID/TIRZ/TIF areas
  • Specialized infrastructure impact analysis

Market Study Visualization

  • Drone and ground photography of the project site
  • Photography and mapped visuals of key competitors
  • Community ecosystem mapping (Work, Play, and Services)

Highest and Best Use

Determine the highest and best use real estate or mix of real estate based on opportunities in the trade area. This includes an analysis of subtypes within the real estate type, and potential timing on what stage to introduce specific types to the market.
